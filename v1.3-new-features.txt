Traveller World & System Generator — What's New in v1.3.0
==========================================================

MOON ORBITS — ECCENTRICITY AND INCLINATION
-------------------------------------------
- Significant moons with orbital positions now have eccentricity and inclination
  rolled using the same tables as world orbits (WBH pp.27–28). Both values appear
  in the Ecc/Incl column of the orbit table moon sub-rows, formatted as
  "0.123/45.6°".

- A moon inclination greater than 90° indicates a retrograde orbit (the moon
  orbits opposite to the planet's rotation), which is now implicit from the
  angle rather than a separate yes/no flag.

- Eccentricity and inclination are emitted in JSON output (as orbit_eccentricity
  and orbit_inclination) when non-zero.

NATIVE LIFE — BIOMASS RATING
------------------------------
- Biomass ratings are now generated for all terrestrial worlds and moons in a
  system when "System detail" is enabled. A "Biomass N" note appears in the
  Notes column of the orbit table for any world or moon with a positive rating.

- The mainworld's biomass rating is always shown in the World Body detail card
  when "Mainworld detail" is enabled (regardless of whether life is present).

- New optional rule: enable "Oxygen requires biomass" to ensure that any world
  with an oxygenated atmosphere (codes 2–9, D, E) has at least biomass 1, even
  if the dice roll would produce no life. The checkbox appears in the generator
  controls alongside "System detail" and is off by default.

WORLD PHYSICAL DETAIL
----------------------
- Surface tidal amplitude is now calculated and displayed in the World Body
  detail card (e.g. "Tidal amplitude: 30.84 m"). This is the combined tidal
  effect from the primary star and all significant moons acting on the world's
  surface.

- Tidal Stress Factor is now shown in the World Body detail card. This is the
  seismic stress contribution from surface tidal forces (tidal amplitude in
  metres ÷ 10, rounded down). It contributes to the Total Seismic Stress.

- Seismic stress is now displayed as three distinct components in the World Body
  detail card:
    • Tidal Seismic Stress — contribution from the world's orbital eccentricity
    • Tidal Stress Factor — contribution from surface tidal forces
    • Residual Seismic Stress — contribution from the world's age, size, and density
  Total Seismic Stress is the sum of all three. Where these differ from the base
  mean temperature, the Seismic Temperature is also shown.

SYSTEM MAP & ORBIT TABLE
------------------------
- The mainworld row in the orbit table is now always displayed in bold,
  including when the mainworld is a satellite of a gas giant.

- The "Orbital Eccentricity" and "Orbital Inclination" checkboxes have been
  removed. Both values are now always calculated when "System detail" is
  enabled, so the Ecc/Incl column is always populated when detail is generated.

BUG FIXES
---------
- Fixed a crash that occurred when generating physical detail for a belt
  mainworld in a system that also had moon tidal effects to apply.
